How to Use Invoice Generator

Making Invoices: It's easy! Fill out the invoice template with all the required details. The invoice editor will show you a preview of the resulting invoice. Once you've filled in the necessary information, you can download or send the invoice. Sending Invoices: You can send invoices to customers as e-invoices through the Invoiced network. The invoices are securely stored in the cloud, and you can accept various payment methods that suit your customer's convenience. Payments: If you use the "send invoice" feature, you can accept payments via bank accounts and credit/debit cards. You can also add payment instructions for alternative payment methods. Downloading Invoices: Click the "Download Invoice" button to get a PDF of your invoice. If you make a mistake, don't worry, you can edit the invoice by clicking "Edit this invoice." Your downloaded or sent invoices are automatically saved to your device's local storage for easy access and editing.

Customizing the Invoice Template

If you frequently use Invoice Generator, you can customize your invoice template. After making your customizations, click "Save Template" in the right sidebar. Your personalized template will be loaded every time you use Invoice Generator, remembering your preferred: From Address Logo Currency Terms Notes Field Titles

System Requirements

To use Invoice Generator, make sure you have one of the following web browsers: Google Chrome (latest two versions) Mozilla Firefox (latest two versions) Apple Safari (latest two versions) Microsoft Edge (latest two versions) Internet Explorer 11 Your browser should also have the following configuration, which is typically enabled by default: JavaScript enabled Local storage enabled TLS v1.2 or above

Where Invoices are Stored

If you use the "Send Invoice" option, you or your recipient can retrieve the invoice by signing in to Invoiced. If you use the "Download Invoice" option, Invoice Generator uses your web browser's local storage to remember your invoices. We don't keep any copies of your downloaded invoice on our servers. Please keep a backup of each invoice PDF you create, or use cloud-based Invoiced for storage and sending. Clearing your browser history will remove all invoices from Invoice Generator, and they cannot be recovered.
